UNESCO Heritage Sites Around The World
World heritage sites are areas that are recognised as having significant cultural, natural or other forms of notable influence. Awarded by the United Nation-affiliated organisation UNESCO, heritage sites are coveted due to the prestige and status it brings to the host nation of the site. The interactable map below shows the location of each site scattered across the world.
From the map, it can be seen that a high number of heritage sites is concentrated in Europe. This is because of several reasons, such as the condition of the site, the financial and acedemic resources of the site's country, and the institution, UNESCO, which originated in Europe. Though an Eurocentric bias is not likely an intentional result (Reyes, 2019), UNESCO'S lack of transparency regarding its selection process can lead to unnecessary misunderstandings among nations.
